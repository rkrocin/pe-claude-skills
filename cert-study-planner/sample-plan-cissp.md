## CISSP Exam Day Tips

1. **Think like a manager, not an engineer.** When two answers are technically correct, choose the one that addresses risk, policy, or governance first. Technology is a tool, not the answer.

2. **Read the FULL question.** CISSP questions often have qualifiers ("BEST," "FIRST," "MOST important") that change the correct answer. The distractor answers are often correct actions but not the BEST or FIRST action.

3. **Safety of life comes first.** In any scenario involving physical danger, protect people before protecting data or systems.

4. **The CAT format is adaptive.** The test adjusts difficulty based on your performance. If questions feel harder as you go, that is a good sign. Do not panic.

5. **You cannot go back.** Once you answer a question, it is final. Do not overthink. Make your best choice and move on.

6. **Manage your time.** With 125-175 questions in 180 minutes, you have roughly 60-85 seconds per question. If you are stuck after 90 seconds, make your best guess and move on.

7. **The exam tests breadth, not depth.** You do not need to be an expert cryptographer. You need to understand when to apply cryptographic controls and which ones are appropriate for a given scenario.
